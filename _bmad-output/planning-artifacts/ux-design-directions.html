Step 09 / Design Directions

Single-page record of the directions that still matter.

This page is the final documentation artifact for the design direction decision. It keeps only the directions that remain meaningful after the discussion and maps them to content behavior, not just visual tone.

Why one page

The secondary geek lens changes tone, not direction. The structural decision now lives in a single professional-mode page, with the Technical Signal Layer retained as a tone influence rather than as a separate alternate showcase.

Final Decision

Persona-First Homepage TOC wins. Technical Signal Layer informs the tone. Restraint Baseline remains the control reference.

  • Selected: Persona-First Homepage TOC as the chosen homepage structure and journey surface.
  • Retained: Technical Signal Layer as the source of technical voice, metadata emphasis, and authored tone inside the chosen structure.
  • Fallback: Restraint Baseline as the calm control reference if the result becomes too directive or too stylized.
  • Excluded: Other exploratory directions were discarded because they increased density or introduced unnecessary alternate framing.
